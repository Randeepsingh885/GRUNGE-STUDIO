We Started This Because
We Were Angry

In 2019, our founders left their cushy agency jobs after watching client after client get handed the same recycled templates with different logos slapped on. The work was insulting. The results were worse.

So we started Grunge.Studio with one rule: every project gets designed from scratch, for that specific audience, with conversion as the north star. Not "vibes". Not "aesthetic". Numbers.

Six years later, we have 340+ shipped projects, a 98% client retention rate, and a very strong opinion about purple gradients.

Work With Us →
6 Years of Brutalism

Design is a weapon.
Most people use it wrong.

Pretty doesn't pay bills. Engagement doesn't buy groceries. What matters is whether a human being, faced with your product, takes the action you need them to take. Everything else is decoration.

We build with intention. Every font choice, every color, every pixel of whitespace — or deliberate lack of it — is in service of one thing: making your audience feel what they need to feel and do what you need them to do.

That's not art. That's engineering. We just make it look brutal while we do it.

What We
Believe

// Values
01

Results Over Pretty

A beautiful website with a 90% bounce rate is a failure. We optimize for outcomes, not awards — though we win those too.

02

Brutal Honesty

We'll tell you your current design is broken. We'll tell you your copy is weak. We'd rather lose a deal than lie to a client.

03

No Templates

Every project starts on a blank canvas. We research your audience, your market, and your competitors before we touch a tool.

04

Speed & Quality

We move fast. Not recklessly — methodically. Two-week sprints mean you see real work, not mood boards, within days.

05

Own The Outcome

We don't ghost after delivery. We track numbers, we iterate, we stay involved until the metrics are where they need to be.

06

Craft Matters

Even within brutalism, craft is everything. The grain overlay is at exactly 18%. The shadow offset is precisely 6px. Details win.

Our
Journey

// Timeline
2019

Founded

Two designers, one MacBook, and a rented basement in Lajpat Nagar. First client: a D2C streetwear brand. First result: 320% conversion increase.

2020

Survived the Chaos

The pandemic hit. We pivoted to help 40+ small businesses get digital. Revenue tripled. Hired 3 more people. Moved to a proper office.

2021

The Grunge Manifesto

Published our design philosophy publicly. It went viral on Twitter. 2,000+ inbound inquiries in 6 weeks. Had to build a waitlist.

2022

International Clients

First US and EU clients. First six-figure project. Moved to a bigger office. Team grew to 8 full-time creatives.

2023–25

Full Throttle

340+ total projects. 11-person team. Launched our design system product. Featured in Design Week India and Creative Review.
